This Privacy Policy explains how Lalyuna ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects any personal information you may provide while using this portfolio website compliant with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may only disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to:

  • Object to the processing of your personal information;
  • Suspend, withdraw, or order the blocking, removal, or destruction of your personal information from our system;
  • Claim compensation if you suffered damages due to inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained or unauthorized use of personal data.
